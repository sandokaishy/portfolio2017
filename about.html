Hi I’m Sheng. I’m a designer based in Taiwan.

I began my design journey when I was a junior in apartment of advertising. Though I wasn’t graduate from design college, the eagerness of making things better drives me to try various design in different fields. That’s why I call myself “Hunger driven” designer.

I became UI/UX and Product design because I was the first and only designer in a start-up team. Web development skill help me create solid user interfaces and being able to do the visual parts of the product during the sprint. The thinking of advertising make me dig the insight deeply, plan for features of the product widely.

It’s just a start. I’m still on the road of UX design. I’m still hungry, hungry for better designs to make things better.
