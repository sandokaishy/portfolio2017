Graphic Design

About this project

Developing the community for engineers(programmers) is the mission of Sudo Recruit. Inspired from the terminals and editors, corporate and identity of Sudo Recruit comes from Ubuntu font family. The underline part of C.I. is also inspired from the cursor of terminal where the codes are created, and it's infinite value of engineers.

Concepts

Sudo, as known as substitue user do or superuser do.
We use the Ubuntu font well-known by programmers, and cut the ascender of d. It makes the identity looks more rational.

Color Strategy

Color strategy derives from themes of "Sublime", fluorescent green and deeper blue.

Identity Guideline

We can stretch the background as we want, but there are minimal space between background edge and the C.I. part when shrinking.

There are space(x height of the C.I typeface) between types and the background edge.

Typography

Sudo Recruit is a kind of Software as a Service. We take SourceHans Sans as our main font family on the website.

Application - Namecard

Lettepress printing makes our namecard. It's really a pity that we couldn't apply fluorescent green on our namecard because of the budget.
