Treepoint Prototype

Date | Oct. 2019
UI/UX Design | Sheng Pan
Prototyping | Sheng Pan
Tools | Sketch, ProtoPie, Lottie

Treepoint will be the brand new point through the Cathay group in the future. MyRewards app is the first place that Treepoint will be located.
The main purpose of this project is to come up with a good way casues less impact to user experience of MyRewards app and combines Treepoint system with it.

Challenge

Reward points is the majority

Most points user do have is the Reward points issued by Cathay United Banks. However, being a new born baby, Treepoint is still weak at fisrt. As a result, coupon exchange with total value of two different points is required in this project.

I built a high-fidelity prototype to make sure whether users understand this idea or not. In addition, check if it will affect the experience or not.
