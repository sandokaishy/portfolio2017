Design Concept

Why do I always get invitations in card style? If wedding is a big campaign in life, the invitation will be the campaign poster.

No more traditional cards made with wedding photography. Instead of traditional card, Wei Hsuan created wonderful illustration for them. The cards is printed with the illustration of bride and groom, made with letterpress technique.
